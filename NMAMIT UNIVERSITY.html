ABOUT NMAM INSTITUITION OF TECHNOLOGY, NITTE


Our Campus

NMAM Institute of Technology, established in 1986, is a premier engineering college under NITTE University. The campus is located at Nitte, Karkala, and offers state-of-the-art facilities, excellent faculty, and a student-centered learning environment.

The institution is committed to academic excellence, research, and holistic student development, preparing graduates to excel globally.
